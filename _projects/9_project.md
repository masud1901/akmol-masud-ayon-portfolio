**FedDQN-TSR** applies **Federated Deep Q-Network** learning to resource-aware traffic sign recognition in Internet of Vehicles (IoV) environments.

### Key Contributions

- Federated RL framework where vehicles collaboratively train a DQN without sharing raw sensor data
- Resource-aware scheduling that adapts to vehicle compute and bandwidth heterogeneity
- Demonstrates convergence and accuracy improvements over centralized and naive FL baselines
- Published at **ICECTE 2026** (IEEE)
